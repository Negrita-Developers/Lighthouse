We Gather Every Sunday

We are a group of ordinary people that serve an extraordinary God.

Our goal is to make disciples of all nations as commanded in
MATTHEW 28:19-20

The only way to get to know Jesus is to begin with what he said about himself. That is why we gather every week to pray to Jesus, sing about Jesus, and to hear teaching from the Bible about how God is accomplishing his plan to rescue the world through Jesus.

Our Church

Peter Chege
Pastor

We desire a strong, Gospel Lighthouse with a burden to reach the world for Jesus through evangelism, fellowship, discipleship, and church planting endeavors around the world after the model of the early church in Acts.
Join us as Kenyan-born Missionary/Pastor, Peter Chege, with his family, and leadership team, lead this dynamic, Gospel-centered church that truly cares for you. All are welcome. Our Services are Bilingual with a blend of English Swahili and African spiritual songs and dance.
If we can be of any spiritual help to you, do not hesitate to call on us.
A Servant of God,
Rev. Peter N. Chege
